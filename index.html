From Bean To Cup:

The process of latte making

Selecting Coffee Beans

Medium or dark roast beans produce the best quality espresso

Grind Level

Espresso requires a fine grind size

Pulling a Shot of Espresso

The ideal espresso shot has a dark brown body with a golden brown crema resting on top

Pouring a Latte

Empty the espresso into a cup and add steamed milk. Enjoy!
